Job description

We are currently looking for a **Data Analyst & Automation Manager (d/f/m) ** to join our team ‘Performance & Contracting - Logistics & Repair Operations’ in Hamburg - Fuhlsbüttel, Germany.

We are a team consisting of Data Analyst & Automation Managers, Process & Performance Managers as well as Airbus Operating System Experts. You will truly be part of a global, multi-cultural and multi-disciplinary team with representation in Denmark, Germany, Singapore and soon in the US.

**DESCRIPTION OF THE JOB **

Data & analytics is one of the largest levers Satair has for accelerating a continuous Improvement culture within the organization. It serves as a vital tool to increase efficiency, competitiveness, employee engagement & most importantly customer satisfaction.

We are looking for our new Data Analyst & Automation Manager who is ready to roll up their sleeves and help us develop actionable, data-driven insights and solve business problems, using various quantitative tools and core project & stakeholder management skills. The candidate will challenge the status quo as well as ensure operational excellence by speeding implementation across the organisation.

Prior experience of developing and managing E2E On-Time Delivery Data Products will be an advantage.

This role reports to the Head of Performance & Contracting and requires continuous cross-functional collaboration with Operations, Sales, Product Management, and IT Teams from all our sites in the world.

**WHAT’S IN IT FOR YOU **

Here is a selection of some of the benefits we offer our employees. Local benefits may vary.

  • Competitive Remuneration
  • Work / Life Balance: Hybrid Working, flex-time, maternity/paternity leave, job sharing
  • Personal Development: Personalized development plans, large portfolio of learning solutions & lots of internal mobility opportunities
  • Health & Wellbeing: Health insurance, well-being programs

**PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITIES **

  • You will manage, structure and implement data sources especially related to Satair On-Time Delivery for performance management and derive insight in how the company performs and can improve
  • You will develop Data Products with adherence to various Satair Data Product Development & Management processes.
  • You will develop performance dashboards with key metrics reflecting company performance and performance perceived by customers for review with senior leadership and business teams.
  • You will deploy statistical techniques and tools for tactical deep dives and analysis; generating actionable insights and effectively communicating recommendations to senior leadership
  • You will act as an internal consultant and supporter for end to end improvement missions
  • You will run/lead data driven root causes analysis and align different functions around a common improvement goal
  • You will translate customer satisfaction expectations into operational target

This role may involve some travel for business mainly within Europe and as such you must be able to travel accordingly.

**ABOUT YOU **

  • You hold a degree in Computer Science, IT, Data & Automation or similar paired with professional experience as a Data Scientist, Data Analyst, Data & Automation Manager or comparable.
  • You have experience in Python, including data manipulation and analysis libraries.
  • You are familiar with database systems and SQL.
  • You are familiar with Palantir Foundry or similar platforms such as Databricks Data Intelligence Platform, Microsoft Fabric etc.
  • You are able to visualize and communicate data and analyses (Tableau or similar).
  • You have a Strong Process Mindset - E2E ‘Order to invoice’ process understanding as the Data Products will be based on such processes.
  • You bring exceptional analytical skills to structure ambiguous situations and you use data-driven troubleshooting to drive solutions in industrial environments.
  • You are a proactive networker, bringing the ability to build long-term stakeholder relationships while confidently challenging and influencing others to drive change.
  • You bring the ability to deliver end-to-end solutions in complex environments, meeting tight deadlines with agility and resilience.
  • You are used to working in multifunctional and multicultural organizations.
  • You have good communication skills (both verbal and written) in English with the ability to explain complex concepts in a simple manner. And any other language is a plus.
