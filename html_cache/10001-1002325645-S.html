Stellenbeschreibung

Do you have an engineering background and experience in the offshore wind or maritime industry? Would you be motivated to be a key person in securing optimal vessel logistics? If so, explore this unique opportunity to grow your career at Vestas, the global leader in sustainable energy.

NCE > Customer Project Execution, NCE > Operations Logistics Offshore

You will be part of the Operation Logistics Offshore department, a specialist team dealing with aviation, ports, crew transfer, accommodation and walk-to-work vessels in Northern and Central Europe. The department is optimizing, analyzing and controlling the vessel logistics throughout the customer process and are working across several teams and departments to support vessel-related activities.

Responsibilities 

  • Establish KPIs and continue development of vessel Daily Progress Reports tracking performance
  • Support the ongoing Vestas vessel operations
  • Operational alignment of vessels starting on new projects
  • Guide Sales, Construction and Service functions in vessel related service assignments and liaise with internal and external service and project staff
  • Support during BIMCO contract negotiations and tenders for service vessels
  • Evaluation of ports and overall logistical concepts
  • Manage reduction of emissions on the chartered fleet of Crew Transfer vessels and Service Operation Vessel with vessel owners and internal stakeholders

Qualifications

We think that you have the below listed experience for this role: 

  • Engineering (mechanical/civil/marine) or nautical degree
  • Experience within offshore wind or maritime industry preferred
  • Good project management skills
  • A contractual and commercial understanding and experience in validating new products and concepts
  • In-depth knowledge of vessel designs, vessel-related inspection, vessel safety standards and marine operations
  • Experienced in data management, primarily Power BI and Excel
  • Proficient English skills, verbal and written

Competencies

To thrive in this role (m/f/d), we believe that you recognize yourself in the following: 

  • A capability and great interest in data analysis
  • A commercial mindset linking business performance and outcomes
  • Delivers results both self-directed and through team collaboration
  • Enjoys and is capable of working in international environments
  • Communicates with clarity, confidence, and purpose
  • Takes initiative for making progress and is motivated to develop
  • Able to schedule, prioritize and follow-up tasks

What we offer

We offer an exciting job with great opportunities for development in an inspiring environment with a world leading wind turbine manufacturer. You will become part of an international environment working from different offices but focused on Northen and Central Europe. You will have opportunities to share experiences, get support and help supporting other Vestas marine specialist colleagues on a global level.
We stand by the values of initiative, accountability, and precision, and you will work with a supportive and capable team that collaborates and assists one another across multiple specialist areas.

Sie werden Teil der Abteilung Operation Logistics Offshore, einem spezialisierten Team, das sich mit Luftfahrt, Häfen, Crew-Transfer, Unterkünften und Walk-to-Work-Schiffen in Nord- und Mitteleuropa beschäftigt. Die Abteilung optimiert, analysiert und steuert die Schifflogistik im gesamten Kundenprozess und arbeitet bereichsübergreifend mit verschiedenen Teams zusammen, um schiffsbezogene Aktivitäten zu unterstützen.

Aufgaben

  • Etablierung von KPIs und Weiterentwicklung von täglichen Fortschrittsberichten zur Leistungsüberwachung der Schiffe
  • Unterstützung der laufenden Schiffsbetriebe von Vestas
  • Operative Abstimmung von Schiffen bei Projektstarts
  • Beratung der Bereiche Vertrieb, Bau und Service bei schiffsbezogenen Aufgaben und Zusammenarbeit mit internen und externen Projektmitarbeitenden
  • Unterstützung bei BIMCO-Vertragsverhandlungen und Ausschreibungen für Serviceschiffe
  • Bewertung von Häfen und logistischen Gesamtkonzepten
  • Management der Emissionsreduzierung bei gecharterten Crew-Transfer-Schiffen und Service-Operations-Schiffen in Zusammenarbeit mit Schiffseignern und internen Stakeholdern

  • Erfahrung in der Offshore-Wind- oder maritimen Industrie von Vorteil
  • Gute Projektmanagementfähigkeiten
  • Vertragliches und kommerzielles Verständnis sowie Erfahrung in der Validierung neuer Produkte und Konzepte
  • Tiefgehendes Wissen über Schiffdesigns, schiffsbezogene Inspektionen, Sicherheitsstandards und maritime Operationen
  • Erfahrung im Datenmanagement, insbesondere mit Power BI und Excel
